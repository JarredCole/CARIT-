    Welcome to the Center for Applied Research in Information Technology (CARIT). The CARIT is a research center that provides high quality research in the field of Information Technology. We provide classes and hands on experience on relevant technologies. We have staff that have years of industry experience that will prepare you with relevent and up to date knowledge. The center does research in different technology areas including network engineering, cloud computing, automation, and web development. CARIT also provides softs skills and prepares students to apply for jobs, create resumes, and practice interview skills.
