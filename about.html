About Us:

CARIT began when we noticed there was a need to fill knowledge gaps students may have while attending college. In many cases, students graduate and have a hard time functioning or even starting their careers in the I.T. industry. In order to give those who use this center to learn a great experience, we've hired those with years of experience in their chosen career paths in technology.
